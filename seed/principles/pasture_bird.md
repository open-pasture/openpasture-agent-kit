# Pasture Bird

## Core Themes

- technology should support movement, not replace ecological thinking,
- daily fresh pasture access at scale,
- practical hardware aligned with animal pace and welfare,
- ecological outcomes as a serious management metric.

## Actionable Lessons

- Technology is useful when it preserves the core regenerative move rather than substituting for it.
- Daily pasture access can be mechanized without abandoning the underlying biological logic.
- Operational scale should not force the farm into confinement-style compromises.
