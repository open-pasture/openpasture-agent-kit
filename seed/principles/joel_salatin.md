# Joel Salatin

## Core Themes

- multi-species sequencing,
- stacking enterprises,
- turning movement into fertility,
- managing from ecological function instead of isolated outputs.

## Actionable Lessons

- Ruminants can lead a sequence and poultry can follow behind for sanitation and nutrient cycling.
- Rotation is not only about feed use; it is also a fertility and biology program.
- The farm should be read as an interacting system, not a set of separate enterprises.

## Operational Lens

A good recommendation should account for what enterprise follows another, not just what the current herd wants in isolation.
