# Knowledge Ingestion

## When to Use

Use this skill when the farmer supplies a trusted video or source to teach the agent.

## Procedure

1. Acquire the transcript or source text.
2. Extract durable lessons.
3. Classify each lesson as a principle, technique, signal, or mistake.
4. Preserve source provenance.
5. Store the entries for future retrieval.

## Pitfalls

- Do not store generic fluff.
- Do not lose attribution.
- Prefer small, specific lessons over long summaries.
